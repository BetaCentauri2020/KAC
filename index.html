HOD's Message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science & IT, Kendrapara Autonomous College, Kendrapara. It gives me immense pleasure to lead the department. Our college is one of best Aided College in the state of Odisha which has accredited by NAAC with A grade two times. This is the Only college to offer B.Sc. Computer Science course as an Honours subject in Undergraduate level. The department is working very hard towards the goal of providing innovative and quality education with good standard to achieve academic excellence in the field of Computer Science. The prime objective of the department to impart quality education in the field of Information and Technology as well as to inculcate the moral and ethical values among the students. We want the education imparted to our students should be the basis of a life time of learning. Learning is a continuous process. The students are given a strong foundation in computer science and problem-solving techniques and are adaptable to changes with a strong emphasis on context and project based learning. The department is proud to have a strong alumni network, many of whom are in good positions in leading Technology industries and Academia both in state and National level. We look forward to having talented students join us who have passion for learning Computer Science at Undergraduate level. The department also encourages the students to pursue higher studies in National and International level institutions. We are confident that our students will emerge as assets not only to the institution and to the organization they belong, but also to the state as well. With this, I welcome you to be a part of our journey towards being a centre of excellence in Computer Science education.

Dr. Ananda Ch. Nayak

M.Sc., PhD, HOD

ABOUT US

The Department of Computer Science & IT was established in the year 2007 with 32 seats at intermediate level. Later in 2010 the Department Of Computer Science got the concurrence to offer Computer Science course as a pass subject in undergraduate level. Considering the growth and significance of the subject, the department got the approval to run B.Sc Computer Science (Hons) from the session 2011-12. The course runs as a self financing course with dully approved by the Department of Higher Education Govt. of Odisha. The Department has established its creditability and has proven its excellence with the honours subject. The department has dedicated faculty members who are committed to impart quality technical education and to develop innovative skills among the students to enrich the academic activities through continual improvement in the teaching and learning process. Our students have proven the academic excellence by taking admissions into higher studies in reputed state and central universities as well as getting placed into MNCs like Infosys, TCS, Mind tree etc. with good packages. The strength of the department is its alumni members. The alumni members of the department are in respectable positions in various leading IT Industries and contribute for the development of the department.
